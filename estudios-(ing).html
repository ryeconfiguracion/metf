STUDY OF ENERGY EFFICIENCY

METF Iluminación Performs studies of energy efficiency in the field of lighting for companies, businesses, institutions and individuals to determine lighting levels, conversion and energy savings in lighting installations both nationally and internationally.

ADVISORY SERVICES

Energy efficiency projects (energy audits).

Audit of energy efficiency in workspaces and buildings

Evaluation of lighting and light measurements in work areas in offices, production and manufacturing sheds, food processing and storage.

Measurements and rectification of lighting in sports fields such as tennis courts, soccer, foosball, baseball, basketball, volleyball, Jailbait and open parking lots.

I develop the design of luminaires for each environment, given the case that does not exist in the market and give solution to the project, such as the restoration of luminaires.

RULES:

Venezuelan in lighting COVENIN, INPSASEL, LOPCYMAT and National Electric Code of Venezuela.

American lighting standards IESNA (Illuminating Engineering Society of North América) & Lighting Handbooks.

CIE, International Commission of illumination as the Spanish rules.

European standards ELI Efficient Lighting Initiative.

Green Building Standards and Certification Systems.

(Sustainable construction certification system) U.S. Green Building Council (USGBC).
