CORPORATE OFFICES OF ALFONZO RIVAS

For this project in 2012 we had to supervise a lighting project carried out by a lighting company, of which we detected several errors and we consulted our client, alerting him of the pro and cons of the lighting project.

In view of the project had excessive lighting for the work plane and luminaires that in our opinion did not meet the type of lighting required.

We decided to make a  a lighting project, together with the design of a 2 x 32W fluorescent luminaire that would be suspended in the slab with curled bar and placed in angles of 30 degrees in the shape of a triangle, without losing sight of the recommended lighting levels for the work plane.

The design of the luminaire was sent to a metalworking company obtaining the best results in terms of its photometry.

At the end a satisfied customer and the human capital that works in the offices at ease with the lighting.
